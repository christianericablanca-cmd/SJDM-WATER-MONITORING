Hello guys,

I built a website for our city — WaterWatch SJDM.

Over the years, water interruptions in San Jose del Monte have been a recurring problem. I created this to help us help each other stay informed and find resources when the water is out.

What you can do:

- Report water issues (no water, low pressure, dirty water, leaks) — anonymously, no account needed
- See all active reports on a live map of SJDM
- Track your report status using your Report ID
- Find water refilling stations, tankers, and laundry services in the directory
- Check emergency hotlines and government contacts
- View the current Angat Dam water level
- Switch between English and Tagalog

Built with Next.js, Supabase, and Leaflet. 100% free, no ads.

Website: https://sjdmwater.vercel.app/

Feel free to share and use it. Feedback is welcome.
